B612 is an highly legible open source font family designed and tested to be used on aircraft cockpit screens. Particular attention had been put on maximizing the distance between the forms of the characters, on respecting the primitives of the different letters and on harmonizing the forms and their spacing.

B612 is a 2 weight family with roman and italic styles accompanied by a monospaced variation, B612 Mono.

Designed by intactile DESIGN for AIRBUS in 2012.
